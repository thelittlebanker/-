Why Start Now

Finance rewards the early starters

Most people only discover finance at university. You are starting years ahead.

📈

Graduate salaries of £55k+

Investment banking analysts at top firms start on £55,000–£80,000 in Year 1. Knowing the field now gives you a massive head start.

🎓

Your GCSEs already matter

Maths, Economics, Business, English — the subjects you pick at 14 shape which finance roles you can target at 22. We show you the link now.

🚀

Spring Weeks start at 16–18

Goldman Sachs, JP Morgan, and BlackRock run programmes for Year 12s. Most students don’t know these exist. You will — and you will be ready.

🧠

It’s not all maths

Different finance roles suit different people. Traders need quick thinking. Bankers need communication. Find your fit now.
